# Brand — Caroline Jones / The Bridge Hub

Voice and positioning for marketing pages. Refine with Caroline as the site develops.

---

## Who Caroline is

Caroline Jones — trauma-informed practitioner working with women on nervous system regulation, maternal wellness, and the patterns that keep them stuck. Master's in psychology. Builds in public (`@itscarolinejones`).

## Audience

Women roughly **28–45** who:

- Feel overwhelmed, disconnected, or "not themselves"
- Are curious about nervous system / somatic approaches
- May have tried therapy or coaching before without lasting change
- Want to feel seen before committing to deeper work

Same audience as Bridge Hub screening — marketing speaks **before** they take the assessment.

## Voice

| Do | Don't |
|----|-------|
| Warm, grounded, plain language | Clinical jargon or diagnostic labels |
| Science-meets-culture (credible + relatable) | Guru tone or overselling |
| Trauma-informed — choice, safety, pacing | Urgency tactics or fear-based marketing |
| Specific about who this is for | Vague "everyone welcome" |

**Reference:** `@itscarolinejones` Instagram — reels and carousels set the tone.

## Positioning

Caroline helps women understand their nervous system patterns and take the next step toward feeling like themselves again — starting with a free screening (Bridge Map) and optional deeper work via the Bridge Programme.

## Locked CTAs (sitewide)

1. **Primary:** Watch the free class → `/free-class`
2. **Secondary:** Discover my profile — free → `/begin` via `SCREENING_START`
3. **No "Book your Clarity Call"** on landing or Bridge Map (results screen only)
