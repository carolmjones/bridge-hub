## PAGE BRIEFS

### Page 2 — The Bridge Map Landing Page
- Structure: Natasha Kiemel regulation profile quiz page
- URL: `/bridge-map`
- Primary CTA: Start the screening
- Key mechanic to explain: Two-tier results. Summary on screen immediately. Deep dive after booking Clarity Call.
- Tone: Curious, warm, credible. Makes her want to know her profile.

### Page 3 — Free Class Page
- URL: `/free-class`
- Content: Video embed (Life Beyond Survival Mode)
- Secondary action: Email capture (joins Kit nurture sequence)
- Tone: Simple. Let the video do the work.

### Page 5 — Clarity Call Booking Page
- URL: `/clarity-call`
- Content: Cal.com embed + short framing copy
- Key message: This is a clinical conversation, not a sales call. Caroline will have read your results.
- Note: Only accessible after completing The Bridge Map.

### Page 6 — Booking Confirmation Page
- URL: `/booking-confirmed`
- Content: You are booked. What happens next (3 steps). Full results on their way.
- Tone: Warm, clear, no pressure.

### Page 7 — About
- URL: `/about`
- Content: Caroline's full story. Qualifications woven in, not listed. Personal healing path. Why she built The Bridge Hub.
- Tone: First person. Direct. Human.

### Page 8 — Work with Me
- URL: `/work-with-me`
- Content: Three services on one scroll.
  - **Coaching** — The Bridge Programme. 8 weeks. 1:1.
  - **Trauma Informed Business Consultancy** — TBD with Caroline.
  - **Keynote Speaking** — TBD with Caroline.
- Tone: Professional but warm. Same voice as the landing page.

### Page 12 — Discovery Call Booking Page
- URL: `/discovery-call`
- Content: Cal.com embed + short framing copy
- Key message: 15 minutes. No commitment. Just a conversation.
- Tone: Softest entry point on the site. No pressure.

### Page 13 — 404 Page
- URL: `/404`
- Content: Short. On brand. Gentle redirect back to home or The Bridge Map.
- Tone: Warm, not clinical. Does not feel like an error.
