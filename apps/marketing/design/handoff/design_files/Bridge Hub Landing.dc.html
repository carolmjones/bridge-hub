The missing piece

Most approaches start with more of everything — more discipline, more mindset work, more pressure. But if your body still feels unsafe, pressure does not close the gap. It widens it. The missing piece is not more effort. It is understanding what your body has been protecting.

How the 8 weeks unfolds

Your transformation, phase by phase

Phase 1 — Weeks 1 and 2

Understanding your nervous system profile

  • Make sense of what your Bridge Map revealed
  • Understand why your body has been responding this way
  • See clearly why this was never a discipline problem

Phase 2 — Weeks 3 and 4

Building the foundation

  • Start working with your nervous system, not against it

Phase 3 — Weeks 5 and 6
